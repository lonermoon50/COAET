about the department

The Department of Soil & Water Engineering was established in 1993. The department deals with undergraduate and postgraduate teaching, research and extension activities. Its aim is to promote academic excellence in the field of Soil and Water Engineering. The courses at the UG and PG level have been designed to equip the students with the knowledge of hydrology, crop water requirement, irrigation methods, groundwater, drainage and soil & water conservation as well as advanced fields of micro-irrigation, GIS and remote sensing etc. The department also conducts research under different fields of Soil and Water Engineering pertaining to farmersâ€™ problems and advancement in the field. .
