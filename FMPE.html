about the department

The Department of Farm Machinery and Power Engineering (Earlier called as Department of Farm Power & Machinery) was established in August, 1993 with starting of M.Tech. (Agril. Engg.) Specialization in Farm Machinery and Power Engineering. The doctoral programme in the department has been started since 2016. The department deals with undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral teaching, research and extension activities. Its aim is to promote academic excellence in the students in the field of Farm Machinery and Power Engineering. The courses at the UG, PG and PhD level have been designed to equip the students with the knowledge of design, development and maintenance of farm machinery and tractor. In addition to courses related to Farm Machinery and Power Engineering, department also deals with testing of farm machineries for assurance of quality implements to the farmers.The name of department was changed from Farm Power and Machinery (FPM) to Farm Machinery and Power Engineering (FMPE) during the year 2013.
