about the department

The Department of Renewable & Bio-Energy Engineering was established in the year 2017. The department deals with undergraduate and postgraduate teaching, research and extension activities. Its aim is to promote academic excellence among students in the field of renewable energy and its application in the field of agriculture. The courses at the UG and PG level have been designed in such a way to equip the students with the knowledge of renewable and bio-energy engineering.
